Technothon
Name of the Event Coordinator: Dr K P N V Satyasree
Venue:  CPLAB2


Technothon is a Coding event conducted on the 1st dep of the fest. This event is focused to enhance the problem-solving skills of the student. This event is designed to test the basic knowledge in C Programming Language and ranging to the complex problems.
This event consists of two rounds : -
• Round 1: This round consists 30 multiple choice questions on Basic C Programming. Language and the time limit for this round is 30 minutes.
• Round 2: The students who are qualified for 2nd round, will be given 3 small algorithms. The fastest coding first with less compile time and execution time win the show.
RULES:
• Only individual participants are allowed for this event.
• The participant must register before entering. The registration fee for this event is 100/-
• A computer will be provided to each participant.
• The exchange of any written code or any participant found talking to other participants will not be encouraged.

F.A.Q

  • What is a yoUR Fest?

    A yoUR Fest is an event organized by a college or university to celebrate student achievements and provide a platform for cultural, artistic, and intellectual expression.

  • How can I participate in a yoUR Fest?

    You can easily select the branch and select the event you want to participant then fill the google form, you can pay the registration fee on the fest dep at tthe college!

  • Is there a dress code for a yoUR Fest?

    There is no specific Dress code for the yoUR fest 2023

  • Are yoUR Fests only for students?

    yoUR Fest are only for the students, and the participants of the other college students are also allowed

  • How much do tickets for a yoUR Fest typically cost?

    The cost of tickets for a yoUR Fest will depend on the specific fest and the activities being offered. Check with the organizers or consult the fest website for more information.

  • What kinds of activities are typically offered at a yoUR Fest?

    College fests may offer a variety of activities such as concerts, workshops, competitions, and exhibitions.

  • Is it necessary for students to carry their ID cards?

    Yes, it is necessary for students to carry their ID cards.
